Introduction to Bears

Bears are large, powerful mammals found in various parts of the world. They belong to the family Ursidae and are known for their distinctive features, such as their stocky build, large paws, and sharp claws.

Bear Species

There are several species of bears, including the polar bear, grizzly bear, black bear, and panda bear. Each species has its unique characteristics and habitats.

Conservation of Bears

Due to various factors like habitat loss, climate change, and poaching, bears are facing conservation challenges. Efforts are being made to protect and preserve bear populations worldwide.
